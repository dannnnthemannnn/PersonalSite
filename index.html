About me

My name is Danny and I am a Software Engineer currently working at Google. Before that I worked at Microsoft and got my master’s degree in software engineering at Stanford. I currently reside in New York City though I was previously located in Seattle. Originally I hail from the great state of Missouri.

I like to think of myself as a full stack developer though there are many who might say I’m flattering myself. This blog contains anything and everything I want to write about with an emphasis on software projects. The opinions expressed here represent my own and not those of my employer.
